### SHOULD-FIX

**S1 — `notesCarryAppCapProvenance` regex prescribes undocumented phrasing**  
`/no manufacturer maximum.*publish/i` rejects "no manufacturer-published maximum", "manufacturer has not published a maximum speed", or any synonym. The required phrasing is implicit — nothing in the spec, error message, or template documents it. A researcher writing a semantically equivalent sentence parks with no actionable explanation.

**S2 — URL extraction in `notesCarryManufacturerCitation` captures trailing punctuation**  
Regex `https?:\/\/[^\s)\]}>,]+` does not exclude `.`, `:`, `;`. A line ending `...https://creality.com/products/k2-se.` extracts the URL with the trailing period; `canonicalSource` normalises to `creality.com/products/k2-se.` — no match. False negative on a correctly cited URL followed by prose punctuation.

**S3 — `notes` error message misleads when root cause is missing field metadata**  
`notesCarryManufacturerCitation` emits `"notes must include a manufacturer citation URL"` when `fieldPasses` fails on `source`/`confidence`/`evidenceType` being absent — not just when the URL is missing from the text.
