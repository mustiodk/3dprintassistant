Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

3D Print Assistant is a hobby project developed by Musti. This policy explains what data the app and website collect and why.

What we collect

The iOS app uses Sentry (sentry.io) to automatically collect crash reports and performance diagnostics. This includes:

  • Crash reports and stack traces — to identify and fix bugs
  • Device model and iOS version — to reproduce issues on specific devices
  • App version — to identify which version a crash occurred in
  • Performance traces — collected on approximately 10% of sessions

This data is sent to Sentry's servers and governed by Sentry's Privacy Policy.

The website uses Cloudflare Web Analytics for anonymous, privacy-preserving traffic measurement. No cookies are used and no personal data is collected.

How data is used

Crash and diagnostic data is used solely to identify and fix bugs. It is not sold, shared with advertisers, or used for tracking of any kind.

Data retention

Crash data is retained in Sentry according to Sentry's standard data retention policy. You can request deletion by contacting the developer.
