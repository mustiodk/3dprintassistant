## Codex (review)

The Round 9 edits are present, but two ratification-level gaps remain.

### Must-fix

1. **P1 — Native pending-identity poisoning.** A stolen `link_intent` can submit or race a different provider callback into the single `pending_identity` slot; the victim’s legitimate Bearer session and PKCE verifier could then finalize the attacker’s identity. The “stolen intent without finalize” test misses this scenario. Bind finalization to a callback-specific, one-time receipt and make pending writes immutable/atomic; test attacker callback → victim finalize and callback races.
